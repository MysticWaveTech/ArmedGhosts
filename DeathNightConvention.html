Death Night Convention

Embrace the journey towards the afterlife with strategy and understanding

Opening Ceremony

Join us as we open with a ritual to acknowledge mortality and embrace the journey ahead. Engage with spiritual leaders from various traditions as they share their insights on death and the afterlife.

Workshops and Panels

  • Afterlife Strategies: Explore teachings and practices from diverse traditions like the Tibetan Bardo and Egyptian beliefs about the afterlife.
  • Mindset Preparation: Learn techniques to face fears, cultivate acceptance, and embrace calm as part of the transition process.
  • Legacy Creation: Guidance on leaving a meaningful legacy, writing living wills, and managing important end-of-life documents.
  • Conscious Dying Techniques: Practice meditation, breathwork, and visualization exercises to mentally prepare for the journey.

Practice Areas

  • Meditative Spaces: Quiet zones for reflection on life and death, including guided meditations simulating afterlife journeys.
  • Virtual Reality Experiences: Use VR to simulate out-of-body experiences, helping attendees confront and accept the transition.
  • Personal Reflection Booths: Record messages for loved ones or self-reflect on life and mortality.

Interactive Storytelling & Legacy Art

Share personal stories, honor loved ones, and contribute to collective art pieces representing your views on life, death, and legacy.

Panel Discussions

  • Philosophical and Scientific Perspectives: Join experts discussing theories of consciousness and the afterlife from philosophical and scientific viewpoints.
  • Spiritual Rituals: Learn about end-of-life practices from diverse faiths and traditions.

Evening Ceremony

Conclude the night with a candlelight or lantern release, symbolizing the cyclical nature of life and death. Embrace the transition together in this powerful closing ritual.
